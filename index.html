XMN


The Metronotes Token

Metronotes is a Multichain Deflationary Token. XMN provides its holders with the simple tokenomics of earning and burning 1% of each transaction. 90% of the XMN Supply is in liquidity on each respective chain with no planned airdrop.

The development team has elected to use only the natural built in burn functionality of the XMN Smart Contract. No hype and no fluff is the intended result of not wasting the supply for the Metronotes Community. Moreso, the Metronotes Team did too much when it Burned Bitcoin in 2015.

Official XMN Deployments:

  • Binance

  • Polygon

  • Harmony

  • Fantom

  • IoTeX
  • Reef
  • Callisto

XMN was created to allow Open Participation in Development of the Metronotes Network. The Metronotes Network is a Layer One Candidate for Tetcoin. The Platform consists of the Metronotes Blockchain, Metroswap, VistaDAO, Vega, and Xynthe. As components of Metronotes are released, holders of XMN may burn their tokens in exchange for holdings of respective protocol. However, anyone can burn XMN at any time for any reason. Burn Away.
